Welcome to Nepal !

Trekking Team Group , a certified tour operator by Travelife and FTA, brings 30+ years of experience to prioritize efficient ground logistics. Our unique approach goes beyond ordinary tourism, immersing travelers in genuine experiences that connect them with the country, its culture, people, and wildlife.

In our commitment to community support, we exclusively employ local guides, delight in local cuisine, and select accommodations owned by locals, including homes, lodges, or hotels.
